Why NekoBeats?

Floating Bars

Fullscreen visualization that floats above everything without interrupting your workflow

Click-through Mode

Enable click-through so the bars won't block your mouse clicks. Use PC normally

Custom Themes

Full color picker - customize bars to match any color you want

Adjustable Settings

Control bar count, height, opacity, and more from the control panel

60 FPS Performance

Smooth, responsive visualization with better performance than V1

Easy Controls

Bar Color

Change bar color to any color you want with the color picker

Opacity

Make bars more or less transparent based on your preference

Height

Adjust how tall bars grow when reacting to audio

Bar Count

Choose between 32-512 bars for different detail levels

Click Through

Toggle whether bars block mouse clicks or not

Draggable

Move the visualizer window around your screen

Troubleshooting

No bars showing?

• Make sure audio is playing through your default output

• Check that your audio isn't muted

Visualizer laggy?

• Reduce bar count in settings for better performance

• Close other intensive applications running in background

Can't click through?

• Make sure to enable "Click Through" in control panel

• Verify no other apps are forcing focus
